 FOUNDER'S STORY

Building the Future of Education in Bangladesh

Growing up in Bangladesh, I witnessed firsthand how access to quality exam preparation resources determined who got ahead and who fell behind. Students in rural districts had little access to well-structured question banks or guided practice exams. Paraffin was born from a simple belief: every Bangladeshi student deserves world-class exam preparation tools, regardless of their background or location.
